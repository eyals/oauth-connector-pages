Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Eyals Lab is an integration used to connect Google services (including Gmail and Google Calendar) for private automation workflows.

What data is accessed

  • Gmail metadata/content needed for email checking and automation workflows
  • Google Calendar events needed for scheduling and reminders
  • Basic account metadata required for OAuth authentication

How data is used

  • To provide automations for the authorized account owner
  • To read and process relevant messages/events for reminders and summaries
  • To support user-requested actions (such as drafts, checks, or scheduling)

Data sharing

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with third parties except as required to operate Google APIs and the user’s own connected automation tools.

Data retention

Retrieved data may be stored locally in private runtime files/logs needed for ongoing automation. Data can be removed by deleting local runtime data and revoking OAuth access.

Security

OAuth credentials and tokens are stored in private local files and used only for authenticated API access. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect access.

Your choices

  • You can revoke access anytime in your Google Account permissions settings.
  • You can stop use of this connector at any time.

Contact

For questions about this policy or data handling, please contact the app administrator.
